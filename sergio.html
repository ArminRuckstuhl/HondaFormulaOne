Sergio Perez

Sergio Perez is undoubtably one of the best drivers in Formula One today. Hailing from Mexico, Checo initially debuted in Formula One in 2011 with Sauber (now Alfa Romeo Racing) following a successful career in GP2 that boasted two victories. We are very much looking forward to working with Sergio in the upcoming years.

In 2020, Checo achieved his best ever finish in the World Drivers Championship (WDC) while racing for BWT Racing Point (now Aston Martin F1), coming forth, just behind now-teammate Max Verstappen. This was in no doubt due to his amazing performance during the event filled Bahrain Grand Prix when he had a miraculous race when he started last after a restart but then managed to overtake every other driver on the grid, eventually finishing first. His first ever victory in Formula One.

This was not the only major event that occured during the Bahrain Grand Prix however, near the start of the race, driver Romain Grosjean had a crash which was one of the worst ever in modern Formula One when he span off the track and went through a crash barrier, the car breaking in half in the process. He was luckily okay and managed to escape the flame and walk to the medical car on his own accord.
