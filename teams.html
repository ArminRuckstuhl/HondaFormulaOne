Our Teams

Beginning in 2019, Honda Formula One replaced Renault as the sole engine supplier for both Red Bull Racing Honda and Scuderia Alpha Tauri. In the last few years, all four cars have seen great success with many wins and podiums. One of the most notable being Pierre Gasly's miraculious victory at the Italian Grand Prix at Monza in 2020 while racing for Scuderia Alpha Tauri.
